Contracting opportunities in affordable housing.

The Department of Empowerment, Compliance, and Opportunity’s (“DECO”) mission is to promote equality of economic opportunities for Minority and Women-owned Business Enterprises (“MWBEs”), Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Businesses (“SDVOBs”), and historically disadvantaged small businesses and to eliminate barriers to their participation in New York State affordable housing contracting.
